About Me

Hello! I'm Aritra Basu, a passionate photographer with over 10 years of experience capturing life's most precious moments. My journey in photography began with a simple curiosity about the world around me, and it has evolved into a profession that I truly love.

I specialize in diverse photography genres including portrait, landscape, and event photography. My approach combines technical expertise with artistic vision to create images that tell compelling stories and evoke emotions.

Whether you're looking for professional headshots, stunning wedding coverage, or breathtaking landscape images, I'm here to bring your vision to life with creativity and dedication.
